Why Join The Meet?

Socializing Your Dog


It's a very important part of your dogs life. Allowing your dog to be free and roam around with other dogs not only allows it to get the exercsise it requires but also mentally stimulates them.


Learning About Sighthound Behaviours


Being around other sighthound owners will help new sighthound owners to understand all their quirky ways compared to different breeds.


Making New Connections


Not only does your dog make new friends but also the owners make new connections. A lot of the owners arrange to look after each others dogs.. which is pretty handy if your thinking of going away.


Exercise


After having such an enthralling time on the walk you won't even realise the amount of exercise you've achieved yourself.
